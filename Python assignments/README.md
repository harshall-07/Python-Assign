Building discipline is about making choices that align with your long-term goals, especially when motivation is low. It acts as a bridge between your aspirations and their actual achievement. 

# Core Principles of Discipline

Consistency over Intensity: 
Success is the result of small efforts repeated daily, not a single massive act.

Choosing Between Wants: 
Discipline is the act of choosing what you want most over what you want now.

Habit Formation: 
Motivation may get you started, but it is habit and discipline that keep you growing.

Self-Mastery as Freedom: 
True freedom comes from being the master of your own mind and actions
